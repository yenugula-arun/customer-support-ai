Customer Refund and Billing Policy

Refund Eligibility
- Refund requests are accepted within 30 days of purchase.
- Requests submitted after 30 days are generally not eligible.
- Refund approval depends on company policy and transaction verification.

Refund Processing
- Approved refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days.
- Processing time may vary depending on the customer's bank or payment provider.

Subscription Policy
- Subscription upgrades take effect immediately.
- Downgrades apply during the next billing cycle.
- Cancelled subscriptions remain active until the current billing period ends.

Non-Refundable Items
- Customized services.
- Professional consulting services already delivered.
- Subscription periods that have already been consumed.

Duplicate Charges
- If a customer reports being charged twice, verify the payment history.
- Duplicate or accidental charges should be reviewed by the billing team.
- If confirmed, the additional charge may be refunded according to the refund policy.

Failed Payment but Amount Deducted
- Customers should wait up to 24 hours for automatic reconciliation.
- If the payment remains unresolved, create a billing investigation ticket.

Invoice Requests
- Customers can download invoices from their billing dashboard.
- If unavailable, support may provide a copy after verification.

Billing Disputes
- Any disputed payment requires manual review by the billing team.
- Customers may be asked to provide transaction details.
