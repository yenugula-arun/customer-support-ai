Customer Support Refund Policy

Eligibility:
- Customers can request a refund within 30 days of purchase.
- Refund requests after 30 days are not eligible.

Processing Time:
- Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Exceptions:
- Subscription fees already consumed are non-refundable.
- Customized services are non-refundable.

Contact:
For refund-related issues contact billing@customersupportai.com
