Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Use the Forgot Password option on the login page.

Q: How long do refunds take?
A: Approved refunds are normally processed within 5 to 7 business days.

Q: I was charged twice.
A: Duplicate charges should be reported immediately. The billing team will verify the transaction and process a refund if an additional charge is confirmed.

Q: My payment failed but money was deducted.
A: Wait up to 24 hours for payment reconciliation. If the amount is not reversed, contact support with your transaction details.

Q: Can I upgrade my subscription?
A: Yes. Subscription upgrades are available at any time.

Q: Can I cancel my subscription?
A: Yes. Cancellation prevents future renewals. Current billing remains active until the subscription period ends.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: Create a support ticket through the support portal.

Q: How can I obtain an invoice?
A: Invoices are available from the Billing section of your account.
