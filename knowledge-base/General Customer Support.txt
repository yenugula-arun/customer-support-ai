General Customer Support Policy

Support Communication
- Always respond politely, professionally, and empathetically.
- Acknowledge the customer's concern before providing a solution.
- Use clear and concise language.

Information Accuracy
- Never provide information that is not available in the company Knowledge Base.
- Do not promise refunds, approvals, discounts, or exceptions unless company policy allows them.

Unknown or Unsupported Requests
- If the issue is not covered by existing documentation, inform the customer that additional investigation is required.
- Request any relevant information needed to continue the investigation.
- Inform the customer that the support team will follow up as soon as possible.

Escalation
Escalate requests involving:
- Duplicate billing disputes.
- Large refund requests.
- Security concerns.
- Account ownership verification.
- Technical issues that cannot be resolved using documented procedures.

Customer Experience
- Maintain a respectful and professional tone.
- Thank customers for their patience.
- Offer additional assistance whenever appropriate.
