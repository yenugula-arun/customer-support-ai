Account Management Guide

Account Creation
- Users must register with a valid email address.
- Email verification may be required before account activation.
- Duplicate accounts using the same email address are not permitted.

Password Reset
- Customers can reset their password using the "Forgot Password" option on the login page.
- Password reset instructions will be sent to the registered email address.
- Reset links expire after 30 minutes for security purposes.
- If the customer does not receive the email, they should check their Spam or Junk folder before requesting another reset.
- If access to the registered email is lost, identity verification is required before updating account credentials.

Account Lock
- Multiple failed login attempts may temporarily lock the account.
- Customers should wait a few minutes before trying again or reset their password.

Profile Updates
- Customers can update their name, phone number, and contact information from Account Settings.
- Email address changes may require verification.

Account Deactivation
- Customers may request permanent account deletion through the support portal.
- Account deletion is irreversible.
- Billing information may be retained where required by company policy or applicable regulations.

Security Guidelines
- Never share passwords or one-time verification codes.
- Support agents cannot view customer passwords.
- Passwords should contain at least eight characters with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.
