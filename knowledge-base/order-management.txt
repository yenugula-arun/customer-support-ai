Order Management

Order Tracking
- Customers can track orders using the Order ID.
- Tracking updates may take up to 24 hours.

Order Cancellation
- Orders can be cancelled before shipment.
- Once shipped, cancellation is no longer possible.

Delivery Delay
- Delays may occur because of weather or courier issues.
- Customers should wait 48 hours before reporting a delayed shipment.

Wrong Item
- Customers should contact support within 7 days.
- Photos may be required.

Missing Package
- Verify the shipping address.
- Check with neighbors or building reception.
- Contact support if still not received after 48 hours.

Address Change
- Shipping addresses can only be changed before dispatch.

Damaged Package
- Customers should upload photos.
- Replacement or refund will be processed after verification.
